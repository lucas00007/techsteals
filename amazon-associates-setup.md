# Amazon Associates Setup for TechSteals

## Step 1: Apply to Amazon Associates Program

### Application Process:
1. **Go to**: https://affiliate-program.amazon.com/
2. **Sign up** with your Amazon account
3. **Fill out application**:
   - Website: https://techsteals.vercel.app/
   - Description: "Tech accessory deal aggregation site"
   - Traffic: 100-500 visitors/month (estimate)
   - Monetization: Affiliate marketing
   - Categories: Electronics, Computers

### Required Information:
- **Tax Information**: SSN or EIN
- **Payment Method**: Bank account or check
- **Website Details**: Your live TechSteals URL
- **Traffic Sources**: Social media, search engines

### Approval Tips:
- ✅ **Quality Content**: Your site looks professional
- ✅ **Clear Navigation**: Easy to use interface
- ✅ **Privacy Policy**: Add one (required)
- ✅ **About Page**: Explain your site's purpose
- ✅ **Contact Info**: Add contact information

**Required text to add to your site:**

**Where to add:**
- Footer of every page
- About page

## Step 4: Generate Affiliate Links

### Link Structure:
```
https://amazon.com/dp/PRODUCT_ID/?tag=YOUR_ASSOCIATE_ID
```

### Example:
```
Original: https://amazon.com/dp/B08N5WRWNW/
Affiliate: https://amazon.com/dp/B08N5WRWNW/?tag=techsteals-20
```

### Amazon Commission Structure:
- **Electronics**: 2.5%

## Step 6: Compliance Requirements

### Must Do:
- ✅ Add disclosure statement

- ✅ Follow content guidelines

### Don't Do:
